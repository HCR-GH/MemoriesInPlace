Record your own memory

Download

Press the record button to start your recording and the stop button when you are done. You can listen back to your recording in the audio player below. If you'd like to re-do your recording simply press record again and your new recording will replace your previous one. Don't forget to allow microphone permissions.

If the audio doesn't start automatically press the play button to listen.

Memories in Place is part of a PhD project at Open Lab working with Remembering the Past
